# Touchline — Player Lounge Guide

Welcome to your Player Lounge! This is your personal hub for everything about your team — upcoming matches, training sessions, coach feedback, live streams, and more.

---

## Getting Started

When you log in, you'll land on the **Home** tab — your dashboard with everything you need at a glance. Use the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen to switch between five sections:

| Tab | What's There |
|-----|-------------|
| **Home** | Dashboard with next match, announcements, quick stats, achievements |
| **Schedule** | Full calendar of matches and training sessions |
| **Live** | Watch live-streamed matches |
| **League** | League table standings |
| **Development** | Your personal feedback and progress |

---

## 1. Availability & Scheduling

### Declaring Your Availability

For every upcoming match, you'll see three buttons to declare your availability:

- **Available** (green) — You can play
- **Unavailable** (red) — You can't make it
- **Maybe** (yellow) — Not sure yet

You can set your availability from:
- The **Next Match** card on the Home tab
- The **Schedule** tab (list view)
- Any **match detail** view

Your selection saves instantly — the coach sees it straight away.

### Viewing Your Schedule

Open the **Schedule** tab to see everything coming up. You can switch between two views:

- **Calendar View** — A monthly calendar with colour-coded dots showing matches, training, and S&C sessions. Tap any day to see its events.
- **List View** — A straightforward list of all upcoming matches and training sessions with dates, times, and locations.

### Adding to Your Calendar

Tap the **calendar icon** on any match to download it to your phone's calendar app. You can also export the full schedule from the calendar view.

### Training Sessions

Each training session shows:
- Date, time, and duration
- Location (tap to open in Google Maps)
- Focus areas for the session
- Whether the coach has uploaded a training plan

Tap a session for full details including warm-up, drills, tactical exercises, and what to bring.

## 2. The Gaffer — Your AI Coaching Assistant

**The Gaffer** is an AI assistant built into your Player Lounge. Think of it as a personal coach you can chat to any time.

### How to Use It

Tap the **"Ask the Gaffer"** button on your Home tab. This opens a chat window where you can type any question. The Gaffer knows about your team, your position, and your development — so answers are tailored to you.

### What You Can Ask

Here are some ideas to get you started:

- *"How can I improve my first touch?"*
- *"What should I focus on in training this week?"*
- *"Can you explain the offside rule?"*
- *"Give me a pre-match warm-up routine"*
- *"How do I deal with nerves before a game?"*
- *"What does my coach's feedback mean about my positioning?"*

### For Parents

Parents can enable or disable The Gaffer from the **Settings** button (cog icon in the header). When disabled, the chat button won't appear in the player's view. All conversations are logged and visible to coaches.

## 3. Communication

### Team Announcements

Important messages from your coach appear at the top of your Home tab. These are colour-coded by priority:

- **Red** — Urgent (read these first!)
- **Orange** — High priority
- **Grey** — General information

Pinned announcements stay at the top so you don't miss them.

### Notifications

Tap the **bell icon** in the header to see your notifications. These include:

- Next match reminders and squad selection
- New achievements and Player of the Match awards
- Upcoming training sessions
- Urgent announcements
- New fixtures added

A red badge on the bell shows how many unread notifications you have.

### Making a Suggestion

Have an idea to make training better? Want to suggest something to the coaches? Tap **"Make a Suggestion"** on the Home tab. You can:

- Choose a category (Training, Communication, Equipment, Schedule, etc.)
- Write your suggestion
- Submit anonymously if you prefer

You can track the status of your suggestions — Pending, In Review, Acknowledged, or Implemented — and see any coach responses.

## 4. Match Day — Pre-Match Prep & Post-Match Reports

### Before the Match

The **Next Match** card on your Home tab is your match-day headquarters. Here you'll find:

- **Opponent and countdown timer** — See exactly how long until kick-off
- **Meeting details** — When and where to meet (tap the location to open Maps)
- **Kit information** — Home or Away strip
- **Match prep notes** — Coach's tactical instructions and team notes
- **Pep talk** — On match day, tap to get a personalised motivational message to get you fired up

### After the Match

Once a match is played, tap it to see:

- **Result** — Score with a Win/Draw/Loss badge
- **Coach's notes** — The manager's thoughts on the game
- **Match report** — A detailed write-up of how the game went
- **Match videos** — Links to Veo highlights or coach-uploaded footage
- **Match photos** — A gallery of photos and videos from the day

---

## 5. Sharing Photos & Media

### Uploading Photos

After a match, you can contribute your own photos and videos. Tap **"Add Media"** on any match card to upload:

- Photos and videos from match day
- Up to 10 files at a time (max 100MB each)
- Drag-and-drop or tap to select files
- Preview your uploads before confirming

### Viewing Media

Photos appear in a gallery grid on the match detail view. Tap any photo to view it full-size. Videos show a play icon on their thumbnail.

## 6. Your Development

### Coach Feedback

Open the **Development** tab to see all the feedback your coaches have given you. Observations are organised by type:

- **Technical** — Ball control, passing, shooting, etc.
- **Tactical** — Positioning, movement, decision-making
- **Physical** — Speed, stamina, strength
- **Mental** — Attitude, communication, leadership
- **S&C** — Strength and conditioning notes

Each piece of feedback shows the date, the match or session it relates to, and the coach's comments.

### Development Plan

Your personalised development plan shows:

- **Strengths** — What you're doing well (keep it up!)
- **Areas to Improve** — What to work on next
- **Goals** — Specific targets to aim for

This is built from your coach's observations and updated as you progress.

### Achievements & Badges

Earn badges and awards throughout the season! Check your achievements on the Home tab or tap to see the full list. These include:

- **Player of the Match** awards (with the reason from the coach)
- Special achievement badges for training and match performances

Your quick stats on the Home tab show your squad number, position, total POTM awards, and coach feedback count at a glance.

---

## 7. Live Streaming

### Watching a Live Stream

Open the **Live** tab to watch your team's matches live. When a stream is active, you'll see a **"LIVE NOW"** indicator and the video player will load automatically.

When there's no live stream, the page will check for updates every few seconds — so just leave it open and it will start playing when the stream goes live.

### Sharing the Stream

Tap the **Share** button to get a link you can send to family and friends. If your coach has set a PIN, that will be included so viewers can access the stream.

You can share via your phone's native share menu or copy the link to your clipboard.

## Tips for Players

- **Check the app the night before a match** for meeting times and kit info
- **Declare your availability early** so the coach can plan the squad
- **Read your coach's feedback** after matches — it's written just for you
- **Use The Gaffer** to ask questions you might feel shy asking in person
- **Upload your photos** after matches to build the team's match-day gallery

## Tips for Parents

- **Check announcements regularly** for schedule changes or urgent info
- **Use the calendar export** to sync fixtures and training with your phone calendar
- **Review your child's Development tab** to see how they're progressing
- **The Gaffer can be toggled on/off** in your Settings if you'd prefer your child not to use the AI chat
- **Share live stream links** with grandparents and family who can't make the game
